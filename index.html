Heart & Blood Pressure

Early signals of hypertension, arrhythmia, and heart failure appear weeks before a crisis. Cardiovascular disease is the #1 cause of death in people over 65 — and the most detectable early.

Respiratory Health

Oxygen saturation and breathing rate catch COPD, pneumonia, asthma flares, and post-COVID complications silently. A 2–3% SpO₂ drop that feels like fatigue can signal serious deterioration.
