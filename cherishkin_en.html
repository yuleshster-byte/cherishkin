CherishKin does not replace a doctor

It helps you see a doctor before it's too late. CherishKin is a monitoring and early-warning tool, not a diagnostic device. When something deviates from baseline — we notify the family. The family notifies the doctor. That's how technology should work in healthcare.

Privacy first

Video is never recorded or stored. All analysis happens on-device in real time. Data is encrypted and never leaves the phone. HIPAA and GDPR compliant.
